Real Shitcoin Price!

Y'all been doing this wrong for long enough. The usual way to calculate a shitcoin price is simply using the price for one unit of shitcoin. This price however is not directly comparable with other shitcoins (or Bitcoin) because everything has a different volume of coins in circulation. You could just look at all the prices in BTC instead of USD, but that's unintuitive for most people and they scream "I have a fundamental misunderstanding of what's happening here so only fiat numbers make sense to me!". OK fine, this site is for you.

Here's how you're meant to do it:

\(rsp = {{c_{s}} \over c_{b}}\times {p_s \over p_b}\)

where:
\(rsp\) is the Real Shitcoin Price in USD
\(c_{s}\) is the current OR final circulation of the shitcoin in question
\(c_{b}\) is the current circulation of Bitcoin
\(p_s\) is the current price of the shitcoin in USD
\(p_s\) is the current price of Bitcoin in USD

Stop comparing apples to watermelons, come on people.
